Event Space

We have a 500 plus capacity Auditorium, a 100 plus capacity conference hall and a 2800 sq. ft Exhibition Hall in the GD Museums campus. These spaces are available to hold themed exhibitions, seminars etc. related to science and technology and also for showcasing museum-on-wheels. The exhibition halls are equipped with all facilities required for hosting events. They are available for rent and to know more, please contact our Admin Manager at 9087300101 or 0422 2222548
