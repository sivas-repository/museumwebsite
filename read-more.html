What We Do?

At GD Naidu Charities, we believe that science should not just be studied—it should be experienced. Founded in 1950 by the visionary inventor and industrialist (Late) Sri G. D. Naidu, the trust has remained steadfast in its mission to foster scientific thinking, innovation, and education. Over the decades, we’ve created platforms that bring science closer to people—regardless of age, background, or education level.

Experimenta Science Centre (Established 2023)

Our latest and most ambitious project, Experimenta, is an interactive science centre spread across 40,000 sq. ft., designed to spark curiosity and promote learning through play. With over 120 hands-on exhibits covering physics, biology, space, energy, robotics, and more, Experimenta lets visitors see, touch, and explore science independently in a joyful and learner-friendly environment. Whether you're a student, a teacher, a parent, or simply curious, Experimenta offers a space where science comes alive in the most engaging and inspiring way.

Gedee Car Museum

An automotive enthusiast’s dream, the Gedee Car Museum is home to 120+ vintage and classic cars, each with its own story. From rare European beauties to historic Indian models, this museum is a tribute to G. D. Naidu’s deep passion for automobiles. The collection spans decades and offers a fascinating journey through automotive evolution. A must-visit for car lovers, historians, and anyone fascinated by design, engineering, and timeless craftsmanship.

GD Science Museum

Dedicated to India’s Edison, the GD Science Museum celebrates the legacy of G.D Naidu’s pioneering inventions. The museum houses India’s:

  • First indigenous electric motor
  • First electric razor
  • Early video games
  • One of India’s earliest personal computers

And a wide range of original tools, electronics, and scientific models Each exhibit reflects the genius and futuristic vision of a man far ahead of his time.
