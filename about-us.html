Our History

The Experimenta Science Centre was founded in the year 2023 by the GD Naidu Charities under the able guidance of Sri GD Gopal, Managing Trustee of GD Naidu Charities. The Trust was set up by (late) Sri GD Naidu, a distinguished scientist, industrialist and philanthropist of Coimbatore, South India, with an objective of setting up museums, science centre and technical institutions, to further the cause of educating the next generation by practical means.

Shri. GD Naidu, well known as ‘the Edison of India,’ was an ardent automobile enthusiast, science lover and loved learning the science behind functioning and invention of everything that he came across. He was a man of practical learning and he strongly believed that empowerment comes through dissemination of knowledge.

Our Mission

We at GD Museums are committed to provide a hub for science and technology, in way of Museums, Science Centre, Laboratories, which aims to enrich the school curriculum and equip the young learners by providing valuable hands-on learning experiences using world class exhibits / technology that enable better understanding of science, technology, engineering, arts and math (STEM) in a fun way. All the museums provide information on technology and the person behind the innovation so as to motivate students to believe anything is possible.

Our Vision

We at GD Museums seek to present the scientific facts and technology in a simple and understandable manner so that people even without basic education can develop their own knowledge and curiosity towards science and engineering. We aim at inspiring the next generation of scientists and engineers.
